Code 2025

Somatic Hypermutation (SHM) Simulator Prototype

A stochastic model of B-cell antibody maturation built in pure Python. Implements a full IUPAC degenerate motif matching engine to identify AID hotspot sequences (RGYW, WRC, GNW), scales per position mutation rates by literature derived enrichment weights and immune cell state, and outputs annotated mutation logs with a hotspot pressure visualization.
